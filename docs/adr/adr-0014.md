# ADR-0014 — Monthly MINOR cadence pinned to upstream OTel Collector MINOR

- **Status**: Accepted
- **Date**: 2026-05-01
- **Deciders**: Tech Lead, Maintainer (V0 planning)
- **Context**: The upstream OpenTelemetry Collector ships a MINOR release every month. Lagging means bug-fix lag and customer-visible delays. Out-running upstream means accepting churn faster than enterprise change-management cycles can absorb.
- **Decision**: Conduit ships a MINOR release every month, pinned to one upstream OTel Collector MINOR. PATCH releases ship on demand for CVEs and customer-blocking bugs. The wire-compatibility window with OTel Collector gateways is a floating ±6 months of the pinned MINOR.
- **Alternatives considered**:
  - Quarterly MINOR releases (rejected: bug-fix lag becomes painful).
  - Continuous main-tracking releases (rejected: out-runs customer change-management).
  - Pin to LTS-style upstream (rejected: upstream OpenTelemetry Collector has no LTS line).
- **Consequences**: Customers expect monthly upgrades; the release pipeline must be reliable enough to ship monthly. Customers who skip MINORs get a documented 6-month rolling upgrade path, beyond which we may require sequential MINOR upgrades.
